Who I am & how I work

I’m an AI & IT undergraduate student in South Korea who likes turning messy, manual processes into clear, small tools. I’m especially interested in customer support workflows, student tools, and automation around data – things that people actually use every day.

I usually start with a simple diagram or text description of the flow, then build it using tools like n8n, PHP & MySQL and APIs. I enjoy asking “what can we remove?” as much as “what can we add?” so that the result stays simple.

My goal is to build a career around practical automation – starting with internships and part-time roles where I can learn from real systems and real users.
