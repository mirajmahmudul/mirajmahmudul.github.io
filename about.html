About
Who I am

I’m Md Mahmudul Hasan Miraj, originally from Bangladesh and currently studying an IT / AI-related major in Busan, South Korea. I’m especially interested in the practical side of technology: how small tools, automations, and clean workflows can reduce friction in everyday work.

Instead of starting with large, complex systems, I focus on “small wins”: automating repetitive steps, organizing information, and making it easier for people to do the work that actually matters. I enjoy thinking through a process, spotting bottlenecks, and designing simple solutions.

My current stack includes automation platforms, PHP & MySQL for small web tools, and basic Python. I’m early in my journey, but serious about learning, building, and getting closer to real-world problems.
