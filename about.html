About

Who I am, where I’m from, and how I like to work.

From Bangladesh to Busan

I grew up in Bangladesh and moved to South Korea to study in an international environment and challenge myself. Adapting to a new language, culture, and education system has made me more independent and careful in how I communicate.

I’m currently studying global IT / AI related subjects while exploring automation, backend development, and practical tools that solve real problems for students, creators, and small teams.

How I approach work

  • Start small: I prefer to build small, focused tools before big systems.
  • Understand the process: I ask “how do you do this now?” before designing a workflow.
  • Write things down: I document the logic so others can understand or extend it later.
  • Keep learning: I constantly experiment with new APIs, automation platforms, and ideas.
